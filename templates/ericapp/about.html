About Us

Welcome to Eric Tech Limited

Based in the heart of Kigali, Rwanda, Eric Tech Limited is dedicated to enhancing the tech industry by offering top-quality electronic and IT equipment, expert-led training programs, and pathways for aspiring tech professionals.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower individuals and organizations with the tools, knowledge, and expertise needed to succeed in today’s rapidly evolving tech landscape. We aim to be a catalyst for Rwanda’s tech growth, impacting our industry locally and globally.

Our Vision

To be the leading provider of innovative tech solutions in Rwanda, driving digital transformation and fostering a skilled, tech-savvy community that contributes to the global tech ecosystem.

Core Values

  • Service Excellence: we are committed to providing the highest quality service to our customers.
  • Innovation: we’re always pushing the boundaries of what’s possible with technology. From the latest electronics to cutting-edge IT solutions.
  • Affordability: Eric Tech is proud to offer competitive pricing without compromising on quality.
  • Hardworking: The foundation of Eric Tech is built on dedication and hard work.
  • Reliability: When you choose Eric Tech, you’re choosing a company that you can trust for high-quality solutions and ongoing customer support.
  • Integrity: Building trust through transparency, accountability, and professionalism.
